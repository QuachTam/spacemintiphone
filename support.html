How can we help?

Tell us what happened and we’ll help you get SpaceMint back on track.

Email Support

Photos permission

Open iOS Settings → SpaceMint → Photos and choose the level of access you want to provide.

Google connection

SpaceMint only accesses Google Drive or Google Photos media that you explicitly authorize or select.

Restore purchases

Open SpaceMint Settings and select Restore Purchases while signed in to the Apple Account used for the purchase.

Cancel a subscription

Manage subscriptions from iOS Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions. Billing is handled by Apple.

Deleting content

Review every suggested item before deletion. Cloud content is not automatically deleted by SpaceMint.

Privacy request

Email support@spacemint.app with the subject “Privacy Request.”
